Retail and Service Replenishment Methodology

How does it work

Our methodology

Etablir's Retail Solution's Forecasting & Order Management application employs sophisticated algorithms to generate optimal suggested quantities for inventory procurement. These suggestions are based on highly accurate forecasts of demand. Furthermore, the application empowers users to prioritize orders while accommodating a range of constraints and limitations.

This comprehensive approach supports complex scenarios like multi-level supply chains, replenishment from vendors to distribution centers, and from vendors to individual retail stores. The Auto-Replenishment module within Etablir's Retail Solution leverages precise demand forecasts to automate the determination of shipment quantities. Additionally, it allocates these products strategically within an organizational hierarchy. Remarkably adaptable, this module is capable of supporting various replenishment processes, including those involving constrained inventory, multi-level supply chains, and omni-channel distribution. It can also cater to specific events that trigger replenishment activities.

Streamlining the allocation process across the entire omni-channel retail network is effortlessly achieved using Etablir's Retail Solution's Allocation solution. This sophisticated tool takes into account diverse factors such as the performance of each location and product, the existing stock levels at different points, the distribution of product assortments, and other pertinent business-specific considerations like size patterns or packaging configurations.

Employing an advanced predictive analytics engine, Etablir's Retail Solution delves into every conceivable factor influencing the retail supply chain. The system then formulates optimal inter-store transfer recommendations. This serves the purpose of efficiently moving merchandise from under performing stores to those experiencing high demand and stockouts for the same products.

These recommendations are meticulously calculated, factoring in all associated costs of such transfers. This dynamic system is also useful for scenarios like revamping product assortments, establishing new stores, and consolidating inventory for enhanced efficiency. Guided by the principle of adding value through stock management, Etablir's Retail Solution's Safety Stock optimizer takes a granular approach. It computes the most suitable safety stock levels for individual products by assessing inherent risks, including demand volatility, variability in lead times, and vendor fill rates. Furthermore, this calculation incorporates an accurate demand forecast and aligns with company policies, particularly customer service level objectives.

The outcome is a balanced safeguard against vendor uncertainties, overstock situations, and the accompanying costs of excessive inventory holding while securing consumer demand and maximizing sales opportunities.
